The water treatment process in Cypress


Cypress, TX, employs a multi-barrier approach when it comes to water treatment. This is accomplished with cutting-edge equipment and advanced treatment technologies that guarantee only the best quality water. The treatment process starts with huge rotating screens that help remove any large debris. After that, coagulation is achieved by adding alum to the water that attracts and binds the small particles into larger groupings. Softening takes place by adding substances (sodium carbonate, caustic soda, and hydrated lime) that help eliminate calcium and magnesium ions. Solid particles are then removed with the aid of clarifiers and chemicals, such as aluminum sulfate, that trigger flocculation. The final outcome is pure, crystal-clear water fit for human consumption and use in other industries too. This process is indicative of the importance given to sustainability in Cypress.
